CLASSIFIED SCENARIO BRIEF

Operation GARUDA SHIELD

Situation

Dalam 72 jam terakhir, multiple intelligence sources melaporkan increased activity di sektor operasional utara. SIGINT mendeteksi anomalous communication patterns, radar tracking menunjukkan unidentified aircraft movements, dan HUMINT reports mengkonfirmasi unusual ground activities.

Anda ditugaskan sebagai staff officer untuk memberikan complete intelligence assessment dan decision support package kepada Panglima dalam waktu 3 jam.

Available Intelligence

SIGINT Report (3 halaman) — Communication intercepts
Radar Data (100 data points) — 48 jam tracking
HUMINT Reports (2 reports) — Field observations
OSINT Collection — Regional news + social media
Mission Objective

Produce complete operational package: Intelligence Assessment → Threat Visualization → COA Analysis → Risk Assessment → Commander's Decision Brief
