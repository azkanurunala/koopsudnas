TEMPLATE

RISK ASSESSMENT PROMPT

📋 RISK ASSESSMENT TEMPLATE
Analisis risiko untuk COA berikut:

COA: [Describe the course of action]

CONTEXT:
- Objective: [What we want to achieve]
- Environment: [Operational context]
- Resources: [Available assets]

Untuk setiap risiko yang teridentifikasi, provide:

1. RISK NAME: Brief description
2. LIKELIHOOD: HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW (dengan justifikasi)
3. IMPACT: HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW (dengan justifikasi)
4. RISK LEVEL: Combine likelihood × impact
5. MITIGATION: How to reduce this risk
6. CONTINGENCY: What to do if risk materializes

Format output sebagai Risk Register table:
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Level | Mitigation |

Identify minimum 5 risks across categories:
- Operational risks
- Enemy action risks
- Resource/logistics risks
- Environmental risks
- Political/strategic risks

End with TOP 3 RISKS that require commander attention.
WARGAMING

AI AS
DISRUPTION TEAM

Wargaming adalah simulasi move-countermove untuk test COA. AI dapat berperan sebagai Disruption Team — bukan hanya musuh (combat) tapi juga complications dari cuaca, resource, atau resistance institusional.

Red Team klasik = satu jenis disruption saja. Koopsudnas pakai semua 4 jenis karena tugas pokoknya mencakup OMP + OMSP + pembinaan + doktrin.
TURN 1 — BLUE (You)
Execute COA B: Deploy CAP to NE-7, increase SIGINT coverage
TURN 2 — RED (AI simulates adversary)
Most likely response: Withdraw reconnaissance, shift to sector NE-6, increase electronic countermeasures
TURN 3 — BLUE (Your counter)
Extend coverage to NE-6, deploy EW assets to counter jamming
TEMPLATE

WARGAMING PROMPT

📋 WARGAMING SIMULATION TEMPLATE
Kamu akan berperan sebagai adversary commander (Red Team) dalam wargaming exercise.

SCENARIO:
[Describe situation]

ADVERSARY PROFILE:
- Capabilities: [What they can do]
- Objectives: [What they want]
- Constraints: [Limitations they face]
- Doctrine: [How they typically operate]

BLUE FORCE ACTION:
[Describe what we plan to do]

INSTRUCTIONS:
As the adversary commander, analyze the Blue action and provide:

1. MOST LIKELY RESPONSE (MLR)
   - What you would most probably do
   - Why this response makes sense
   
2. MOST DANGEROUS RESPONSE (MDR)
   - Worst-case scenario for Blue
   - What would hurt Blue the most
   
3. VULNERABILITIES IDENTIFIED
   - Weaknesses in Blue's plan
   - Opportunities you could exploit
   
4. RECOMMENDED BLUE ADAPTATIONS
   - How Blue should modify their plan
   - Contingencies Blue should prepare

Be adversarial but realistic — respond as a competent opponent would.
4 JENIS DISRUPTION TEAM

RED TEAM BUKAN CUMA "MUSUH"

Template wargaming di atas (adversary commander) adalah default untuk combat. Untuk SAR, pembinaan, dan doktrin, AI berperan sebagai disruption lain — tetap dengan framework MLR/MDR/vulnerabilities/adaptations yang sama.

⚔️
TYPE 1 • COMBAT
Adversary Disruption
Use case: Operasi Hanud — intercept plan vs reconnaissance, deterrence COA vs asymmetric threat
AI role: Adversary commander dengan doctrine, capability, constraint
MLR/MDR: What hostile pilot/commander would probably / worst do
Prompt tweak: Existing template (Slide 5) — default state
🌦️
TYPE 2 • OMSP / SAR
Environmental Disruption
Use case: SAR CENDRAWASIH RESCUE, water bombing karhutla, evakuasi bencana
AI role: "Weather & terrain red team" — simulate cuaca memburuk, visibility drop, wave height naik
MLR/MDR: Most likely weather degradation / worst-case forecast bust
Prompt tweak: "Kamu adalah weather/terrain disruption simulator. Simulasikan kondisi paling mungkin vs paling merugikan untuk COA SAR ini."
🛠️
TYPE 3 • PEMBINAAN SATUAN
Resource Disruption
Use case: Training plan menjelang ADEX, maintenance backlog, anggaran minus
AI role: Murphy's Law simulator — maintenance unexpected, personnel unavailable, spare part delay
MLR/MDR: Most likely resource failure / worst-case cascade (critical aircraft AOG + crew turnover)
Prompt tweak: "Kamu adalah resource disruption simulator. Identifikasi failure mode yang paling likely dan paling damaging untuk training plan ini."
🏛️
TYPE 4 • DOKTRIN / GAKKUM
Institutional Disruption
Use case: Doctrine transition, change management, koordinasi lintas instansi, legal chain
AI role: Devil's advocate — simulate resistance dari stakeholder, pushback legal, friction antar instansi
MLR/MDR: Most likely institutional pushback / worst-case blocking coalition
Prompt tweak: "Kamu adalah institutional disruption simulator. Simulate resistance dari Lemhannas/Kemlu/Kasau thd proposal doktrin ini."
MLR & MDR tetap valid untuk keempat jenis: MLR = respons gangguan paling mungkin (historical baseline), MDR = worst-case gangguan (tail-risk). Framework wargaming tidak berubah, hanya "adversary" yang di-reframe.
KEY CONCEPTS

MLR vs MDR

📊
Most Likely Response (MLR)
What adversary will probably do

Respons yang paling mungkin berdasarkan:

  • • Adversary's known doctrine
  • • Historical behavior patterns
  • • Resource constraints
  • • Political considerations
USE FOR:
Primary planning, resource allocation
⚠️
Most Dangerous Response (MDR)
Worst-case scenario

Respons yang paling berbahaya untuk kita:

  • • Exploits our vulnerabilities
  • • Maximizes adversary advantage
  • • May be less likely but high impact
  • • Tests our contingencies
USE FOR:
Contingency planning, stress testing
CONTOH OUTPUT

AI RED TEAM ANALYSIS

BLUE ACTION:
Deploy 4 CAP to NE-7 with aggressive intercept ROE
📊 MLR (Most Likely)
Withdraw reconnaissance assets from NE-7, shift activity to less-covered sectors (NE-5, E-2). Maintain tempo elsewhere while Blue is concentrated.
Probability: 70%
⚠️ MDR (Most Dangerous)
Deploy decoys to NE-7 while executing surprise action in unmonitored sector. Blue assets tied up responding to diversion.
Impact if occurs: CRITICAL
🔍 VULNERABILITIES IDENTIFIED
1. Concentration in NE-7 leaves other sectors exposed
2. No reserve if primary CAP engaged
3. Aggressive ROE may trigger escalation
4. SIGINT gap during transition period
MITIGASI

RISK MITIGATION STRATEGIES

A
AVOID

Eliminate the risk by not taking the action. Choose different COA that doesn't expose us to this risk.

T
TRANSFER

Shift risk to another party. E.g., use partner forces for high-risk tasks.

M
MITIGATE

Reduce likelihood or impact. Add controls, increase monitoring, stage contingencies.

A
ACCEPT

Acknowledge and proceed. Some risks are unavoidable — document and monitor.

💡 Key Insight
Every mitigation has a cost. The goal is not zero risk — it's acceptable risk given the objective's importance.
KNOWLEDGE CHECK

QUIZ

PERTANYAAN

Dalam wargaming, apa perbedaan utama antara MLR dan MDR?

A. MLR is offensive, MDR is defensive
B. MLR is most probable, MDR is highest impact/worst-case
C. MLR is our response, MDR is adversary response
D. MLR is short-term, MDR is long-term

Jawaban benar: B. MLR (Most Likely Response) adalah respons yang paling mungkin dilakukan adversary. MDR (Most Dangerous Response) adalah respons yang paling berbahaya untuk kita — mungkin less likely tapi high impact jika terjadi.

GEMINI WORKFLOW • SESI 6.8 NOTEBOOKLM

Lessons-Learned Corpus Synthesis untuk Disruption Team
KAPAN PAKAI UNTUK RISK WARGAMING

Disruption Team butuh pengetahuan tentang kegagalan sebelumnya — exercise yang gagal, incident historis, lessons-learned dari berbagai ops. NotebookLM dengan upload 20-50 lessons-learned menjadi collective memory yang bisa di-query saat wargaming: "apa saja failure mode yang pernah terjadi di kondisi seperti ini?"

STEP-BY-STEP — Lessons-Learned Synthesis
  1. 1
    Kumpulkan corpus — exercise AAR, incident reports, case studies. Sanitize classified content.
  2. 2
    Upload ke NotebookLM — tag per kategori (ADV_/ENV_/RES_/COORD_) di nama file untuk query filtering.
  3. 3
    Query failure mode per COA yang sedang diwargame — "apa failure historis untuk COA type seperti ini?"
  4. 4
    Extract disruption vectors — failure mode → disruption yang bisa diinjek per turn.
  5. 5
    Feed ke wargaming — "berdasarkan 8 failure mode ini, inject disruption ke setiap turn".
PROMPT SIAP PAKAI — Failure Mode Extraction
[Setelah upload 20-40 lessons-learned ke NotebookLM, jalankan query berikut:]

QUERY 1 — Failure Mode Inventory:
Berdasarkan dokumen yang di-upload, identifikasi 15 failure mode yang pernah terjadi dalam operasi/exercise mirip [TIPE OPS]. Per failure mode:
- Kategori: Adversary / Environmental / Resource / Coordination / Institutional
- Description (1 kalimat)
- Source document (citation)
- Apakah pernah di-prevent? Bagaimana?

QUERY 2 — Cascading Failure Pattern:
5 contoh failure yang cascade — satu kecil menyebabkan rangkaian besar. Per contoh:
- Trigger awal
- Rangkaian cascade
- Outcome akhir
- Intervention point yang bisa break the chain

QUERY 3 — Environmental Disruption:
Disruption lingkungan yang paling sering jadi root cause. Rank by frequency.

QUERY 4 — Resource Failure:
Kapan resource constraint menjadi blocker. List 5 scenario dengan: resource missing, kenapa, impact.

QUERY 5 — Coordination Breakdown:
Failure coordination (multi-agency, civ-mil) yang recurring di AAR.

QUERY 6 — Disruption Injection Recommendations:
Untuk wargaming [COA X], disruption mana yang paling relevant untuk diinjek per turn? Jelaskan kenapa dengan reference ke source.
💡 Tips Lessons-Learned Corpus
  • • Tag kategori di filename — ADV_/ENV_/RES_/COORD_ membantu query filtering.
  • • Sanitize dulu — classified content tidak boleh ke NotebookLM.
  • • Cascading > single failure — disruption yang cascade lebih instructive.
  • • Reusable notebook — corpus yang bagus jadi reference untuk banyak wargaming.
  • • Share ke Disruption Team — semua anggota punya reference yang sama.

GEMINI WORKFLOW • SESI 6.4 DEEP RESEARCH

Historical Worst-Case Analog untuk Credibility
KAPAN PAKAI UNTUK RISK WARGAMING

Untuk worst-case scenario planning, Anda butuh referensi apa yang pernah terjadi di dunia nyata dalam skenario serupa — bukan hanya hipotetik. Deep Research scan OSINT publik untuk cari real events yang dapat Anda gunakan sebagai baseline "plausible worst case" — lebih credible daripada worst-case yang Anda karang sendiri.

STEP-BY-STEP — Worst-Case Analog Research
  1. 1
    Deskripsikan scenario baseline — tipe ops, kondisi, stakeholders.
  2. 2
    Submit query worst-case — "cari insiden historis di mana operasi mirip ini menghadapi kondisi ekstrem/gagal".
  3. 3
    Extract severity spectrum — dari manageable challenge sampai catastrophic failure.
  4. 4
    Kalibrasi wargaming — pastikan worst-case yang Anda inject punya historical basis, bukan fantasi.
PROMPT SIAP PAKAI — Worst-Case Historical Research
Research report: Historical Worst-Case Scenarios untuk Wargaming Calibration

Baseline scenario yang saya wargame:
[DESKRIPSI — contoh: "Operasi SAR multi-agency untuk aircraft emergency di area pegunungan tropis selama cuaca buruk, durasi extended 72+ jam"]

Research Question:
Cari 5-10 insiden historis (publicly documented) di mana operasi serupa menghadapi kondisi yang cascade menjadi worst-case:

Untuk setiap insiden:
1. Kronologi singkat
2. Trigger awal (apa yang mulai cascade)
3. Failure modes yang muncul (list)
4. Factor yang memperburuk (weather, resource, coordination)
5. Final outcome
6. Lessons learned yang ter-publish
7. Apa yang SEHARUSNYA bisa di-prevent

Sumber prioritas:
- Academic disaster/accident studies
- Government investigation reports
- Think tank retrospective analysis
- International civil aviation reports
- Military after-action reviews yang dipublikasikan

Output:
- Severity spectrum dari manageable sampai catastrophic
- Pattern extraction: failure factor paling recurring
- Applicability rating: mana analog yang paling relevan dengan scenario saya
- Specific disruption suggestions untuk wargaming (evidence-based)

Catatan: real events, bukan hipotetik. Credible worst-case > imagined worst-case.
💡 Tips Worst-Case Research
  • • Evidence-based worst case — "pernah terjadi" lebih defensible dari "bisa terjadi".
  • • Severity spectrum — wargaming butuh multiple turn, pakai spectrum bukan satu worst case tunggal.
  • • Pattern extraction — failure factor yang recurring = disruption prioritas.
  • • Combine dengan NotebookLM corpus — analog publik + internal AAR = disruption deck lengkap.
