  • Tone: combat, operational urgency

DEFINITION

APA ITU
DECISION BRIEF?

Decision Brief adalah dokumen yang menyajikan semua informasi yang dibutuhkan komandan untuk membuat keputusan — dalam format yang ringkas, jelas, dan actionable.

⏱️
Time-Efficient
Komandan sibuk — brief harus bisa dibaca dalam 5 menit
🎯
Decision-Focused
Bukan info dump — ada clear recommendation
📋
Self-Contained
Semua info yang dibutuhkan ada di satu dokumen
STRUKTUR

7 SECTIONS
DECISION BRIEF

1
BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)
2 sentences max — recommendation + why
2
Situation Summary
What's happening? Threat level, key facts
3
Options Considered
Brief summary of each COA evaluated
4
Recommended COA
Which option + detailed justification
5
Risk Summary
Key risks + mitigations for recommended COA
6
Decision Requested
What exactly does commander need to approve?
7
Timeline for Execution
When action needed? Key milestones
CRITICAL SKILL

WRITING THE BLUF

Bottom Line Up Front — paling penting!

✕
BAD BLUF

"This brief will discuss the situation in Sector NE-7 and present several options for consideration based on intelligence gathered from multiple sources over the past 48 hours."

⚠️ Too vague, no recommendation, wastes time
✓
GOOD BLUF

"Recommend immediate deployment of CAP to Sector NE-7 to deter escalating hostile activity. Decision required by 1800Z to maintain initiative."

✓ Clear recommendation, why, and urgency
💡 BLUF Formula
[Recommendation] + [Key reason] + [Urgency/timeline]
TEMPLATE

DECISION BRIEF PROMPT

📋 DECISION BRIEF COMPILATION PROMPT
Compile the following deliverables into a Commander's Decision Brief:

=== INPUT DELIVERABLES ===

INTELLIGENCE REPORT (from Phase 1):
[PASTE YOUR INTEL ASSESSMENT HERE]

VISUAL PACKAGE (from Phase 2):
[DESCRIBE YOUR DASHBOARD/MAP/SITREP]

COA ANALYSIS (from Phase 3):
[PASTE YOUR COA COMPARISON + WARGAMING RESULTS]

=== BRIEF STRUCTURE ===

Create a decision brief with these 7 sections:

1. BLUF (2 sentences max)
   - Recommendation + why + urgency

2. SITUATION SUMMARY (50 words)
   - Threat level, key facts, confidence

3. OPTIONS CONSIDERED (bullet points)
   - COA A: [one-liner]
   - COA B: [one-liner]  
   - COA C: [one-liner]

4. RECOMMENDED COA (100 words)
   - Which option
   - Why this over others
   - Expected outcome

5. RISK SUMMARY (bullet points)
   - Top 3 risks + mitigations

6. DECISION REQUESTED
   - Specific approval needed

7. TIMELINE
   - Decision deadline
   - Key execution milestones

=== FORMAT REQUIREMENTS ===
- Professional military tone
- No jargon that needs explanation
- Visual: include [REFERENCE: Dashboard] etc where appropriate
- Total length: 1 page max
EXAMPLE

SAMPLE DECISION BRIEF

DTG: 131800Z APR 2026
OPERATION CORAL SHIELD
MEDIUM-HIGH
1. BLUF
Recommend COA B: establish defensive perimeter in Sector E-3 to deter further incursion while avoiding escalation. Decision required by 2000Z to deploy assets before daybreak.
2. SITUATION
Threat level MEDIUM-HIGH. 47 vessel contacts detected in Eastern sectors (+220% baseline). Hostile naval exercise ongoing. Intelligence confidence HIGH (SIGINT confirmed, IMINT partial).
3. OPTIONS
• COA A: Full engagement — clear all hostile contacts
• COA B: Defensive perimeter + deterrence posture
• COA C: Diplomatic pressure + monitoring only
4. RECOMMENDED
COA B scores highest (29/40) in decision matrix. Balances objective achievement dengan minimal escalation risk. Preserves diplomatic options while demonstrating resolve. [See: VISUAL PACKAGE — Decision Matrix]
5. RISKS
• Adversary escalation → Ready QRF
• Asset attrition → Reserve 2 units
• Intel gaps → Continue ISR
6-7. DECISION & TIMELINE
Approve: COA B deployment
Deadline: 2000Z today
Execute: 0400Z tomorrow

QUIZ

PERTANYAAN

Dalam Decision Brief, apa yang HARUS ada di BLUF section?

A. Detailed situation analysis
B. Recommendation + why + urgency
C. List of all COAs considered
D. Complete risk assessment

Jawaban benar: B (Recommendation + why + urgency). BLUF adalah "Bottom Line Up Front" — komandan langsung tahu apa yang direkomendasikan, mengapa, dan seberapa urgent.

GEMINI WORKFLOW • SESI 6.3 CANVAS

Final Decision Memo — Integrasi Semua Phase
KAPAN PAKAI UNTUK FINAL DECISION BRIEF

Canvas adalah tempat menyatukan semua output dari Phase 1-3 menjadi satu final decision memo yang coherent. Intel fusion (Phase 1), visual package (Phase 2), wargaming report (Phase 3) — semuanya di-refer dan di-synthesize dalam final memo. Canvas memungkinkan iterative refinement section-level sampai memo siap untuk presentation.

STEP-BY-STEP — Final Decision Memo Integration
  1. 1
    Minta final memo skeleton — 9 section yang reference ke Phase 1-3 outputs.
  2. 2
    Decision Required — 1 kalimat eksplisit, apa yang komandan harus putuskan setelah capstone presentation.
  3. 3
    BLUF 5 bullet — compressed summary dari semua Phase output, siap dibaca dalam 30 detik.
  4. 4
    Evidence Base — intel fusion summary (refer ke Phase 1 NotebookLM output).
  5. 5
    COA Analysis — final COA yang sudah di-stress-test (refer ke Phase 3 wargaming report).
  6. 6
    Q&A Prep — 15 likely questions komandan dengan jawaban pendek.
  7. 7
    Iterative refinement — setiap section direfine berkali-kali sampai memo siap defend.
PROMPT SIAP PAKAI — Final Decision Memo Skeleton
Buat Canvas Final Decision Memo untuk Capstone Phase 4:

Judul: FINAL DECISION MEMO — [TOPIK CAPSTONE]
Klasifikasi: UNCLASSIFIED // FOR TRAINING ONLY
Prepared by: [PLACEHOLDER]
Decision by: Pangkoopsud
Date: [PLACEHOLDER]

Struktur 9-section (integrasi Phase 1-3):

1. DECISION REQUIRED (1 kalimat)
   "Pangkoopsud diminta memutuskan: [X atau Y atau Z]"

2. BLUF — BOTTOM LINE (5 bullet, 30-detik read)
   - Situation (1 bullet)
   - Evidence strength (1 bullet)
   - COA recommended (1 bullet)
   - Confidence + key risks (1 bullet)
   - Decision timeline (1 bullet)

3. EVIDENCE BASE (reference Phase 1 Intel Fusion)
   - Key findings dengan citation
   - Confidence matrix summary
   - Data gaps yang di-acknowledge

4. SITUATIONAL ANALYSIS
   - Threat/opportunity assessment
   - Operational context
   - Timeline pressure

5. COA OPTIONS COMPARISON (tabel markdown)
   | COA | Description | Feasibility | Acceptability | Risk | Resource |
   [3 COA dengan scoring]

6. RECOMMENDATION (refer Phase 3 Wargaming)
   - Preferred COA + justification
   - Why this passed wargaming stress test
   - Residual risk yang di-accept
   - Mitigation plan (reference dari wargaming report)

7. IMPLICATIONS
   - Resource impact
   - Operational impact (0-30 days)
   - Strategic impact (30+ days)
   - Political/reputational impact
   - Ethical/legal considerations

8. IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP
   - H+0 to H+24 (immediate)
   - H+24 to H+72 (short-term)
   - H+72+ (sustained)
   - Decision gates / checkpoints

9. Q&A PREP (15 likely questions)
   Generate 15 pertanyaan komandan:
   - 3 about evidence quality
   - 3 about COA tradeoffs
   - 3 about resource/feasibility
   - 3 about worst case scenarios
   - 3 about political/media implications
   Per question: 2-3 kalimat jawaban yang defensible.

10. APPENDICES
    - A: Intel Fusion Report (Phase 1)
    - B: Visual Package (Phase 2) — reference images
    - C: Wargaming Report (Phase 3)
    - D: Source bibliography

Format: maksimal 4 halaman utama (exclude appendix), A4, formal military Indonesia. Ini adalah deliverable final untuk capstone presentation.
💡 Tips Final Decision Memo
  • • Reference bukan repeat — memo utama concise, appendix yang detail. Jangan duplikasi.
  • • Q&A Prep 15 pertanyaan — capstone defense sering intense. Lebih siap lebih baik.
  • • Maksimal 4 halaman utama — Pangkoopsud time = precious.
  • • Mitigation plan actionable — bukan aspirasi. Tindakan konkret dengan timeline.
  • • Iterative refinement — minimal 3 iterate passes sebelum presentation.

GEMINI WORKFLOW • SESI 6.5 CREATE VIDEO

Final Capstone Video — Dramatic Presentation Element
KAPAN PAKAI UNTUK FINAL PRESENTATION

Capstone presentation yang memorable biasanya punya satu dramatic video moment — bukan banyak, tapi satu yang impactful di saat paling strategis. Biasanya: video sebelum Decision Required slide (untuk emotional weight) atau video di closing (untuk lasting impression). Create Video memberi Anda cinematic quality tanpa videographer profesional.

STEP-BY-STEP — Final Capstone Video
  1. 1
    Pilih moment paling strategis — biasanya sebelum Decision Required atau di closing. Kualitas > kuantitas.
  2. 2
    Concept cinematic yang match topik — bukan generic footage, tapi visual yang carry emotional weight terkait capstone Anda.
  3. 3
    Generate 3-5 variasi dari prompt yang sama, pick yang paling powerful.
  4. 4
    Integrate dengan audio dari Create Music — video + audio sync = memorable moment.
  5. 5
    Test di rehearsal — impact di laptop bisa berbeda di projection room.
PROMPT SIAP PAKAI — Final Capstone Dramatic Clip
Generate 1 dramatic video clip untuk capstone presentation moment (before Decision Required slide):

Concept: Sunrise over vast Indonesian maritime territory, symbolic of "dawn of decision" — commander must choose the path forward.

SUBJECT:
Wide aerial cinematic shot of sunrise breaking over endless ocean dotted with distant tropical islands. Golden orange light breaking through heavy cloud layer. Dramatic cumulus clouds catching first sunlight. Gentle camera forward drift motion creating sense of moving toward horizon. Vast scale conveys territorial space and responsibility.

STYLE:
Photorealistic cinematic quality. Color grading: deep blue pre-dawn transitioning to warm gold as light arrives. Dramatic lighting. 16:9 aspect ratio. Slow thoughtful pace, not fast. Epic but contemplative.

MOOD:
Decision weight, responsibility, hope tempered with seriousness. This is the moment before a significant choice — the atmosphere should feel weighty but not despairing.

TECHNICAL:
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Duration: 5-8 seconds
- NO specific military elements visible
- NO realistic aircraft
- Pure atmospheric establishing shot
- Content filter safe: "aerial", "cinematic sunrise", "maritime territory"

Generate 3 variations. Pick the most impactful.
💡 Tips Final Capstone Video
  • • Satu video impactful > 5 video biasa — pilih moment paling strategis.
  • • Match concept ke topik — generic footage = forgettable. Specific visual metaphor = memorable.
  • • Sync dengan audio — video + music simultan = peak impact.
  • • Test di venue rehearsal — projector color/brightness affects perception.
  • • Pacing slow — capstone audience butuh waktu untuk absorb, bukan fast cuts.

GEMINI WORKFLOW • SESI 6.6 CREATE MUSIC

Final Capstone Audio — Signature Sound for Presentation
KAPAN PAKAI UNTUK FINAL PRESENTATION

Audio di capstone final = signature sound untuk dramatic moment. Satu cue yang sync dengan video dramatic (Embed 2) adalah kombinasi paling powerful. Tidak perlu audio throughout — hanya di moment strategis.

STEP-BY-STEP — Signature Audio
  1. 1
    Match mood ke video dramatic — kalau video Anda hopeful, audio harus inspirational. Kalau serious, audio harus contemplative.
  2. 2
    Durasi 30-45 detik — cocok dengan video moment + transisi.
  3. 3
    Structure dengan arc — start subtle, build, peak at video climax, resolve.
  4. 4
    Test sync dengan video — pacing bisa perlu adjust di video atau audio untuk perfect sync.
  5. 5
    Fade-out sebelum Decision Required — audio hilang smooth sebelum komandan fokus decision. Silence = gravitas.
PROMPT SIAP PAKAI — Final Capstone Signature Audio
Generate signature audio cue untuk capstone dramatic moment (sync dengan sunrise video clip):

Style: Cinematic orchestral, contemplative but building
Mood: Decision weight, responsibility, dawn of resolution. Serious but with underlying hope.

Instruments:
- 0-8s: Single sustained string note (contemplative opening, match pre-dawn blue tones in video)
- 8-18s: Piano joins (thoughtful melody line, light)
- 18-28s: Full strings gradually build (as sunrise peaks in video)
- 28-35s: Subtle low brass join + soft choir pad (gravitas)
- 35-45s: Gentle fade to silence (audio hilang saat komandan akan fokus Decision Required)

Tempo: Slow contemplative (55-65 BPM)
Structure: Arc from subtle → build → peak → resolve
Duration: 40-45 seconds
NO vocals, NO lyrics, NO sudden loud hits
Dynamics: Quiet start, peak at middle, gentle fade

Emotional cue: "Moment of decision, weight of responsibility, but hope with the dawn"
Purpose: Plays with sunrise video clip in capstone presentation, fades before Decision Required slide

Technical: High quality stereo, suitable for projector room acoustics
💡 Tips Signature Audio
  • • Arc matters — start subtle, build, peak, resolve. Linear tidak memorable.
  • • Sync dengan video dramatic — audio + video peak simultan = maximum impact.
  • • Fade before Decision Required — silence adalah powerful transition ke moment keputusan.
  • • Test di rehearsal — audio perception berbeda di headphone vs projector room.
  • • Satu signature > banyak background — capstone butuh impactful, bukan ambient.
