Apa itu Deep Thinking?

Deep Thinking (atau Chain of Thought) adalah teknik prompting yang memaksa AI untuk "berpikir step-by-step" sebelum memberikan jawaban. Ini menghasilkan reasoning yang lebih mendalam dan keputusan yang lebih justified.

KEY INSIGHT

"Think before you answer" — dengan Deep Thinking, AI akan menunjukkan reasoning process-nya, bukan hanya memberikan conclusion.

Standard vs Deep Thinking

❌ STANDARD PROMPT

"Apa COA terbaik untuk situasi ini?"

→ Output: Jawaban langsung tanpa reasoning

✓ DEEP THINKING PROMPT

"Analisis situasi ini step-by-step. Identifikasi opsi yang tersedia, evaluasi pro/con masing-masing, lalu rekomendasikan COA terbaik dengan justifikasi."

→ Output: Full reasoning chain + justified recommendation

Deep Thinking: Chain of Thought Methodology

Buka Sesi KONSEP

Chain of Thought Steps

1
UNDERSTAND
Restate the problem in own words
2
DECOMPOSE
Break into sub-problems
3
ANALYZE
Evaluate each component
4
SYNTHESIZE
Combine insights into conclusion
5
VALIDATE
Check logic and assumptions

Trigger Phrases

Kata-kata yang memicu deep thinking:

"Think step by step" "Analyze carefully" "Consider all factors" "Show your reasoning" "Before concluding..." "Evaluate pros and cons"
Kenapa Deep Thinking Penting?

Untuk keputusan strategis, kita butuh tahu MENGAPA suatu rekomendasi dibuat — bukan hanya APA rekomendasinya.

TEMPLATE 01 • Deep Thinking COA Analysis
Kamu adalah [ROLE: strategic military advisor]. SITUASI: [DESCRIBE CURRENT SITUATION] OBJECTIVE: [DEFINE DESIRED END STATE] CONSTRAINTS: - Resources: [LIST] - Time: [DEADLINE] - Rules of Engagement: [CONSTRAINTS] TUGAS: Develop and analyze 3 distinct COAs. THINK STEP BY STEP: 1. UNDERSTAND: Restate the problem and objective 2. GENERATE: Create 3 distinct approaches (conservative, moderate, aggressive) 3. ANALYZE: For each COA, evaluate: - Probability of success - Resource requirements - Risk level - Time to execute - Potential adversary response 4. COMPARE: Score each COA (1-10) on criteria 5. RECOMMEND: Select best COA with clear justification FORMAT OUTPUT: ## Situation Summary ## COA 1: [Name] - Conservative ## COA 2: [Name] - Moderate ## COA 3: [Name] - Aggressive ## Comparison Matrix ## RECOMMENDATION with Justification
TEMPLATE 02 • Risk Assessment & Mitigation
Kamu adalah [ROLE: risk assessment specialist]. PROPOSED ACTION: [DESCRIBE THE PLAN/COA] CONTEXT: [RELEVANT SITUATION DETAILS] TUGAS: Lakukan comprehensive risk assessment. ANALYZE EACH RISK CATEGORY: 1. OPERATIONAL RISKS - Execution failures - Coordination issues - Technical malfunctions 2. ADVERSARY RISKS - Counter-actions - Preemptive strikes - Intelligence compromise 3. ENVIRONMENTAL RISKS - Weather impact - Terrain challenges - Civilian considerations 4. RESOURCE RISKS - Supply chain disruption - Personnel availability - Equipment reliability FOR EACH IDENTIFIED RISK: - Description - Likelihood: [1-5] - Impact: [1-5] - Risk Score: [L x I] - Mitigation Strategy - Residual Risk after mitigation OUTPUT: Risk Register with Top 5 Risks and Mitigation Plans
TEMPLATE 03 • AI Wargaming (Red Team)
Kamu akan berperan sebagai [ADVERSARY] dalam wargame exercise. ADVERSARY PROFILE: - Capabilities: [LIST KNOWN CAPABILITIES] - Doctrine: [TYPICAL TACTICS] - Objectives: [WHAT DO THEY WANT?] BLUE FORCE ACTION: [DESCRIBE OUR PLANNED ACTION] AS THE ADVERSARY, THINK STEP BY STEP: 1. How would you detect this action? 2. What is your most likely response? 3. What is your most dangerous response? 4. What vulnerabilities in Blue's plan would you exploit? 5. What counter-moves would you prepare? PROVIDE: ## Most Likely Adversary Response - Actions - Timing - Forces committed ## Most Dangerous Adversary Response - Actions - Why this is dangerous - Indicators we would see ## Vulnerabilities in Blue Plan - List of exploitable weaknesses ## Recommended Blue Adjustments - How to mitigate adversary responses
TEMPLATE 04 • Commander Decision Brief
Kamu adalah [ROLE: staff officer] preparing decision brief untuk [COMMANDER LEVEL]. DECISION REQUIRED: [WHAT DECISION NEEDS TO BE MADE] BACKGROUND: [CONTEXT AND SITUATION] ANALYSIS COMPLETED: [SUMMARY OF COA ANALYSIS] FORMAT OUTPUT AS DECISION BRIEF: ## BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front) [1-2 sentences: recommendation + why] ## SITUATION (30 seconds read) - Current status - Key developments - Time constraints ## OPTIONS CONSIDERED | Option | Pro | Con | Risk | [Brief table format] ## RECOMMENDED COURSE OF ACTION - What: [Action] - Why: [Justification] - Risk Level: [HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW] - Resources Required: [Summary] ## DECISION REQUESTED [ ] Approve COA [X] [ ] Approve COA [Y] [ ] Request additional analysis [ ] Other guidance ## TIMELINE - Decision needed by: [DATE/TIME] - Execution begins: [DATE/TIME]
