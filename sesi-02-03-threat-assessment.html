GUIDED STEP 1

INITIAL DATA SCAN

Prompt untuk Initial Scan

Sebagai radar analyst, review data berikut dan berikan:
1. Summary statistik (total records, time range, sectors covered)
2. Identifikasi data points yang anomali (deviasi dari baseline)
3. Initial observations

BASELINE:
- Normal sectors: NE-5, NE-6, E-1
- Normal altitude: 30,000-38,000 ft
- Normal type: COMM (commercial)
- Normal count: 3-5 per time slot

DATA:
[paste radar data table]

Expected Output

SUMMARY:
50 records, 24-hour period, 4 sectors (NE-5, NE-6, NE-7, E-1)
ANOMALIES DETECTED:
  • • R05, R08, R11: Sector NE-7 (not in baseline)
  • • R05, R08, R11: Type UNK (unknown)
  • • R05, R08, R11: Altitude 10-15K (below normal)
  • • Count escalating: 8 → 12 → 18
INITIAL OBSERVATION:
Concentrated anomalies in NE-7 sector with escalating pattern
2
GUIDED STEP 2

CROSS-SOURCE CORRELATION

Prompt untuk Correlation

Sekarang korelasikan radar data dengan field report:

RADAR ANOMALIES:
- R05 (0800): NE-7, 8 contacts, 15K ft
- R08 (0930): NE-7, 12 contacts, 12K ft  
- R11 (1100): NE-7, 18 contacts, 10K ft

FIELD REPORT:
[paste field report]

ANALISIS:
1. Apakah waktu dan lokasi match?
2. Apakah field report confirm atau contradict radar?
3. Apa yang field report tambahkan (info baru)?
4. Confidence level dari correlation?

Expected Output

✓ CORRELATION FOUND:
  • • Time match: Field report 1045-1115 aligns with R11 (1100)
  • • Location match: Grid NE-7 confirmed
  • • Altitude match: "Very low" consistent with 10-15K
+ NEW INFORMATION:
  • • Aircraft count: 3-4 (matches radar trend)
  • • Behavior: "Pattern flights" (reconnaissance indicator)
  • • No radio response (evasion behavior)
CONFIDENCE:
HIGH — Multiple sources confirm same activity
3
GUIDED STEP 3

THREAT ASSESSMENT

Final Prompt: Generate Threat Assessment

Sebagai intelligence analyst senior, berdasarkan analisis sebelumnya, buat THREAT ASSESSMENT BRIEF.

CORRELATED FINDINGS:
- Radar: 3 anomaly events in NE-7 (0800, 0930, 1100)
- Activity escalating: 8 → 12 → 18 contacts
- Low altitude (10-15K ft), no transponder
- Field report confirms visual (1045-1115)
- Pattern flights observed, no radio response
- Source reliability: B-2

ASSESS AND PROVIDE:
1. Threat Level (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH) dengan justifikasi
2. Most likely hypothesis tentang aktivitas ini
3. Intelligence gaps yang masih ada
4. Recommended actions (immediate + short-term)

FORMAT OUTPUT:
## BLUF (50 kata)
## Threat Assessment
## Key Evidence
## Intelligence Gaps  
## Recommended Actions

Bahasa Indonesia formal, max 300 kata.

VARIANT EXERCISE (OPSIONAL)

RISK ASSESSMENT LINTAS DOMAIN

Framework 3-step (scan → correlate → assess) yang sama juga bisa dipakai untuk domain non-tempur. Pilih satu variant di bawah untuk coba sendiri setelah Sektor Bravo selesai.

🆘
VARIANT B • OMSP

Humanitarian Risk — CENDRAWASIH RESCUE

Bukan threat ke kita, tapi risk ke keselamatan survivor & keberhasilan misi SAR

SKENARIO:
Gempa M7.2 Banda Sea 0215L. ATR-72 rute Ambon–Kaimana lost contact 0230L (4.2°S 129.8°E, 18,000 ft). 68 pax + 4 kru. Cuaca visibility <2 km. Basarnas request airlift koordinasi.
Sebagai SAR coordinator Koopsudnas,
review 3 sumber berikut dan hasilkan
HUMANITARIAN RISK ASSESSMENT BRIEF.

SUMBER:
1. Radar last track ATR-72 (timestamp,
   heading, altitude, speed drop-off)
2. BMKG weather forecast (visibility,
   wind, wave height, 6-jam outlook)
3. SAR asset availability (C-130, CN-235,
   helikopter, ETA ke search area)

LAKUKAN 3 LANGKAH:
1. Scan: identifikasi data anomali
   (komunikasi terakhir, drift probability)
2. Correlate: match weather + last track
   → estimasi search area prioritas
3. Assess risk:
   - Risk to survivor (time-critical factor)
   - Risk to SAR mission success
   - Risk to rescue aircraft (weather)

FORMAT:
## BLUF (50 kata)
## Risk Level (LOW/MED/HIGH) + confidence
## Prioritized Search Sectors
## Resource Allocation
## Immediate Actions (0-6h)

Tone: humanitarian, multi-agency
(Basarnas/TNI AL/BMKG), bukan combat.
⚖️
VARIANT C • GAKKUM UDARA

Legal Risk — FIR VIOLATION

Risk eskalasi diplomatik + risk chain-of-custody evidence

SKENARIO:
Business jet asing masuk FIR Jakarta tanpa clearance, tidak respons radio, heading menuju ruang udara sensitif. Opsi: force-down prosedur. Koordinasi AirNav + Kemlu.
Sebagai Perwira Ops Koopsudnas
dengan latar gakkum udara, review
3 sumber berikut dan hasilkan
LEGAL RISK ASSESSMENT BRIEF.

SUMBER:
1. Radar track pesawat (registrasi,
   heading, altitude, kecepatan)
2. AirNav clearance log (tidak ada
   filed flight plan, no radio response)
3. Diplomatic context (asal pesawat,
   perjanjian bilateral relevan)

LAKUKAN 3 LANGKAH:
1. Scan: identifikasi pelanggaran
   (ruang udara, komunikasi, transponder)
2. Correlate: radar + clearance + context
   → kategori pelanggaran
3. Assess legal risk:
   - Risk eskalasi diplomatik
   - Risk chain-of-custody evidence
   - Risk prosedural (ROE, UU Penerbangan)

FORMAT:
## BLUF (50 kata)
## Legal Risk Level + confidence
## Escalation Ladder (peaceful → force-down)
## Stakeholder Notifications
   (AirNav, Kemlu, Kejakmil)
## Immediate Actions

Tone: analitis, prosedural,
mengacu UU Penerbangan & ICAO.
Prinsip yang sama: scan → correlate → assess + justification + recommendations. Yang berubah hanya framing risk (threat / humanitarian / legal) dan stakeholder yang dilayani brief-nya.
