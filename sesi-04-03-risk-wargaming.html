TEMPLATE

RISK ASSESSMENT PROMPT

📋 RISK ASSESSMENT TEMPLATE
Analisis risiko untuk COA berikut:

COA: [Describe the course of action]

CONTEXT:
- Objective: [What we want to achieve]
- Environment: [Operational context]
- Resources: [Available assets]

Untuk setiap risiko yang teridentifikasi, provide:

1. RISK NAME: Brief description
2. LIKELIHOOD: HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW (dengan justifikasi)
3. IMPACT: HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW (dengan justifikasi)
4. RISK LEVEL: Combine likelihood × impact
5. MITIGATION: How to reduce this risk
6. CONTINGENCY: What to do if risk materializes

Format output sebagai Risk Register table:
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Level | Mitigation |

Identify minimum 5 risks across categories:
- Operational risks
- Enemy action risks
- Resource/logistics risks
- Environmental risks
- Political/strategic risks

End with TOP 3 RISKS that require commander attention.
WARGAMING

AI AS
RED TEAM

Wargaming adalah simulasi move-countermove untuk test COA. AI dapat berperan sebagai adversary — memprediksi respons dan membantu identify vulnerabilities.

TURN 1 — BLUE (You)
Execute COA B: Deploy CAP to NE-7, increase SIGINT coverage
TURN 2 — RED (AI simulates adversary)
Most likely response: Withdraw reconnaissance, shift to sector NE-6, increase electronic countermeasures
TURN 3 — BLUE (Your counter)
Extend coverage to NE-6, deploy EW assets to counter jamming
TEMPLATE

WARGAMING PROMPT

📋 WARGAMING SIMULATION TEMPLATE
Kamu akan berperan sebagai adversary commander (Red Team) dalam wargaming exercise.

SCENARIO:
[Describe situation]

ADVERSARY PROFILE:
- Capabilities: [What they can do]
- Objectives: [What they want]
- Constraints: [Limitations they face]
- Doctrine: [How they typically operate]

BLUE FORCE ACTION:
[Describe what we plan to do]

INSTRUCTIONS:
As the adversary commander, analyze the Blue action and provide:

1. MOST LIKELY RESPONSE (MLR)
   - What you would most probably do
   - Why this response makes sense
   
2. MOST DANGEROUS RESPONSE (MDR)
   - Worst-case scenario for Blue
   - What would hurt Blue the most
   
3. VULNERABILITIES IDENTIFIED
   - Weaknesses in Blue's plan
   - Opportunities you could exploit
   
4. RECOMMENDED BLUE ADAPTATIONS
   - How Blue should modify their plan
   - Contingencies Blue should prepare

Be adversarial but realistic — respond as a competent opponent would.
KEY CONCEPTS

MLR vs MDR

📊
Most Likely Response (MLR)
What adversary will probably do

Respons yang paling mungkin berdasarkan:

  • • Adversary's known doctrine
  • • Historical behavior patterns
  • • Resource constraints
  • • Political considerations
USE FOR:
Primary planning, resource allocation
⚠️
Most Dangerous Response (MDR)
Worst-case scenario

Respons yang paling berbahaya untuk kita:

  • • Exploits our vulnerabilities
  • • Maximizes adversary advantage
  • • May be less likely but high impact
  • • Tests our contingencies
USE FOR:
Contingency planning, stress testing
CONTOH OUTPUT

AI RED TEAM ANALYSIS

BLUE ACTION:
Deploy 4 CAP to NE-7 with aggressive intercept ROE
📊 MLR (Most Likely)
Withdraw reconnaissance assets from NE-7, shift activity to less-covered sectors (NE-5, E-2). Maintain tempo elsewhere while Blue is concentrated.
Probability: 70%
⚠️ MDR (Most Dangerous)
Deploy decoys to NE-7 while executing surprise action in unmonitored sector. Blue assets tied up responding to diversion.
Impact if occurs: CRITICAL
🔍 VULNERABILITIES IDENTIFIED
1. Concentration in NE-7 leaves other sectors exposed
2. No reserve if primary CAP engaged
3. Aggressive ROE may trigger escalation
4. SIGINT gap during transition period
MITIGASI

RISK MITIGATION STRATEGIES

A
AVOID

Eliminate the risk by not taking the action. Choose different COA that doesn't expose us to this risk.

T
TRANSFER

Shift risk to another party. E.g., use partner forces for high-risk tasks.

M
MITIGATE

Reduce likelihood or impact. Add controls, increase monitoring, stage contingencies.

A
ACCEPT

Acknowledge and proceed. Some risks are unavoidable — document and monitor.

💡 Key Insight
Every mitigation has a cost. The goal is not zero risk — it's acceptable risk given the objective's importance.
KNOWLEDGE CHECK

QUIZ

PERTANYAAN

Dalam wargaming, apa perbedaan utama antara MLR dan MDR?

A. MLR is offensive, MDR is defensive
B. MLR is most probable, MDR is highest impact/worst-case
C. MLR is our response, MDR is adversary response
D. MLR is short-term, MDR is long-term

Jawaban benar: B. MLR (Most Likely Response) adalah respons yang paling mungkin dilakukan adversary. MDR (Most Dangerous Response) adalah respons yang paling berbahaya untuk kita — mungkin less likely tapi high impact jika terjadi.
