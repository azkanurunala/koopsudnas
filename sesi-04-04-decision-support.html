=== SCENARIO ===
[Describe the situation requiring decision]

=== OBJECTIVE ===
[What needs to be achieved]

=== CONSTRAINTS ===
- Time: [Deadline]
- Resources: [Available assets]
- Rules: [ROE, political limits]

=== DELIVERABLES ===

PART 1 - SITUATION ANALYSIS (Chain of Thought)
Think step by step:
1. Restate the problem
2. Identify key factors (friendly, enemy, environment)
3. List critical assumptions
4. State information gaps

PART 2 - COURSES OF ACTION
Generate 3 distinct COAs:
- COA A: [Aggressive approach]
- COA B: [Balanced approach]  
- COA C: [Conservative approach]

For each: Description, Key Actions, Pros, Cons, Resource needs

Create comparison matrix (Effectiveness, Risk, Cost, Speed, Flexibility)

PART 3 - RISK ASSESSMENT
Top 5 risks for recommended COA:
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Level | Mitigation |

Include wargaming insight: MLR and MDR

PART 4 - RECOMMENDATION
- RECOMMENDED COA: [X]
- JUSTIFICATION: [Why this is optimal]
- KEY CAVEATS: [Important assumptions/limitations]
- DECISION POINTS: [If X happens, then consider Y]
- NEXT UPDATE: [When to reassess]
CAPSTONE EXERCISE

OPERATION
IRON RESOLVE

📋 Situation Brief

Current Situation
Increased naval activity detected near exclusive economic zone boundary
Intel Summary
3 frigates + 2 supply vessels observed conducting "exercises" 15nm from EEZ line. SIGINT indicates reconnaissance mission. HUMINT suggests 7-day duration.
Objective
Deter violation of EEZ without escalating to armed confrontation

📊 Constraints & Resources

Timeline Decision needed within 12 hours
Available Naval 2 corvettes, 1 patrol vessel
Available Air 4 maritime patrol aircraft
ROE Limit No first use of force
Political Avoid international incident
🎯 Your Mission
Create complete 4-part Decision Support Package dalam 40 menit
📋
PART 1

SITUATION ANALYSIS

1. Problem Restatement
"We need to respond to potential EEZ violation by foreign naval forces while avoiding escalation..."
2. Key Factors
FRIENDLY
2 corvettes, limited air coverage
ENEMY
3 frigates, superior firepower
ENVIRONMENT
Near EEZ boundary, high visibility
3. Assumptions
• Adversary does not intend armed confrontation
• HUMINT source reliability: B-2 (usually reliable)
• Weather will remain favorable for 72h
4. Information Gaps
• Exact mission objectives unknown
• Submarine presence unconfirmed
• Adversary ROE unclear
🎯
PART 2

COA DEVELOPMENT

COA A: SHOW OF FORCE
Deploy all assets to intercept
Full naval + air deployment to EEZ boundary. Visible presence, active tracking.
Max Deterrent High Escalation
COA B: LAYERED PRESENCE
Graduated response posture
1 corvette at EEZ, air patrol active, 1 corvette on standby. Diplomatic note sent.
Balanced Flexible
COA C: OBSERVE & PROTEST
Surveillance only
Air surveillance, document violations, formal diplomatic protest. No naval deployment.
Low Risk Low Deterrent
Preliminary Matrix Result
COA A: 6.2 | COA B: 7.8 | COA C: 6.5 — COA B leads on balanced criteria
⚠️
PART 3

RISK ASSESSMENT

Escalation to armed incident
Mitigation: Clear ROE, hot-line established
HIGH
Adversary ignores presence, crosses EEZ
Mitigation: Prepared escalation ladder
MEDIUM
Equipment failure during deployment
Mitigation: Standby corvette ready
MEDIUM
📊 MLR (Most Likely)
Adversary completes exercise, avoids direct EEZ violation, withdraws after 7 days
⚠️ MDR (Most Dangerous)
Adversary tests response, brief EEZ incursion forces reaction decision
✅
PART 4

RECOMMENDATION

RECOMMENDED: COA B (Layered Presence)
Provides credible deterrence while maintaining escalation control. Diplomatic engagement creates off-ramp. Reserve assets enable response to MDR if it materializes.
KEY CAVEATS
• Assumes adversary intent is not hostile
• Requires diplomatic coordination within 6 hours
• Effectiveness depends on visible air presence
DECISION POINTS
• IF adversary crosses EEZ → Elevate to COA A, notify command
• IF adversary withdraws early → De-escalate to COA C, maintain surveillance
• IF exercise extends beyond 7 days → Reassess with updated intel
NEXT UPDATE
Situation reassessment in 24 hours or immediately if adversary movement detected
