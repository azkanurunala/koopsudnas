SIGINT mendeteksi anomalous communication patterns, radar tracking menunjukkan unidentified aircraft movements, dan HUMINT reports mengkonfirmasi unusual ground activities.

METHODOLOGY

INTELLIGENCE FUSION FRAMEWORK

Gunakan framework ini untuk mengintegrasikan semua 4 sources menjadi unified assessment:

1. CORRELATION MATRIX
Identify apa yang dikonfirmasi oleh multiple sources:
• Location (NE-7/8) — SIGINT + Radar + HUMINT ✓
• Timing (dawn/dusk) — SIGINT + Radar ✓
• Naval presence — HUMINT + OSINT ✓
2. GAP ANALYSIS
Identify apa yang belum diketahui:
• Exact force composition?
• Intent (reconnaissance vs pre-positioning)?
• Duration beyond "7 days"?
3. CONFIDENCE LEVELS
Assign confidence per assessment:
• HIGH: Multi-source corroboration
• MEDIUM: 2 sources agree
• LOW: Single source only
4. THREAT PICTURE
Synthesize into unified assessment:
• WHO is involved?
• WHAT are they doing?
• WHERE is the activity?
• WHEN is the timeline?
• WHY (assessed intent)?

   - Overall confidence: HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW
   - Justify the rating
