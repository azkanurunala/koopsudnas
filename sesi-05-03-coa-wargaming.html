OBJECTIVE & CONSTRAINTS

🎯

Objective

Deter foreign reconnaissance operations in sectors NE-7 to NE-9 while avoiding escalation to armed confrontation.
⚠️

Constraints

• ROE: No first use of force
• Political: Avoid international incident
• Resources: 2 corvettes, 4 MPA available
• Timeline: Exercise ends 22 APR

COA A: ASSERTIVE PRESENCE

High Deterrent High Escalation
COA B: GRADUATED RESPONSE
Layered presence + diplomacy
1 corvette on station, 1 reserve. 2 MPA active, 2 standby. Diplomatic note sent.
Balanced Flexible

Monitor and document only

Low Risk Low Deterrent

AI WARGAMING — COA B

TURN 1 — BLUE (You)
Deploy 1 corvette to NE-7 perimeter. 2 MPA commence patrol. Diplomatic note sent to foreign MFA.
↓ AI Red Team Response ↓
TURN 2 — RED (Adversary MLR)
Acknowledge diplomatic note, cite "routine exercise." Maintain air reconnaissance but shift primary activity to NE-8. Test our coverage gaps.
↓ Your Counter ↓
TURN 3 — BLUE (Counter)
Extend MPA coverage to include NE-8. Position reserve corvette to respond if needed. Share intel with regional partners.

Adversary completes exercise with reduced scope. No EEZ violation. Deterrence achieved.
