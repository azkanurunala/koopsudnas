Source-Grounded Research Tool

  • • Grounded ke sumber — tidak akan menjawab di luar dokumen yang Anda upload
  • • Citation wajib — setiap klaim linked ke dokumen asli (klik untuk verify)

CRITICAL SKILL

SOURCE RELIABILITY FRAMEWORK

NotebookLM tidak otomatis membedakan sumber berkualitas tinggi dan rendah — tanggung jawab ada di operator

Sebelum upload ke NotebookLM, rate tiap sumber menggunakan adaptasi dari NATO Intelligence Source Evaluation (modifikasi untuk OSINT):

KODE SOURCE CREDIBILITY CONTOH AKSI DI NOTEBOOKLM
A1Sangat reliabel, terverifikasiOfficial gov docs, peer-reviewed, primary sourceInclude sebagai core source
B2Reliabel, track record bagusJane's, IISS, CSIS, War on the RocksInclude, mention di nama file
C3Cukup reliabel, perlu corroborateDefense News, The Diplomat, think tanks kecilInclude, flag di nama file [C3]
D4Tidak pasti, unverifiedBlog defense, forum militer, media belum dikenalPisahkan ke notebook terpisah
F6Tidak dapat dipercayaAnonimous, propaganda, social media unverifiedJangan upload ke notebook intel
💡 Naming Convention yang Disarankan
✓ JANES_UAV_2025_A1.pdf
✓ CSIS_INDOPASIFIK_Q3_B2.pdf
✓ BLOG_DEFENSE_ANALYSIS_C3.pdf
Format: [SOURCE]_[TOPIK]_[PERIODE]_[RATING].pdf
⚠ Bahaya Mix Source Tanpa Rating
Kalau upload A1 dan F6 bersama, NotebookLM treat keduanya sama. Klaim dari F6 bisa masuk ke briefing komandan dengan citation seolah valid. Ini yang paling sering menyebabkan intel error dalam konteks AI-assisted analysis.

🗂️ Strategi Notebook Tiering

TIER 1 — HIGH CONFIDENCE
Hanya sumber A1 + B2. Untuk briefing komandan dan produk intel formal.
TIER 2 — WORKING ANALYSIS
A1 + B2 + C3. Untuk analisis kerja, bukan final product. Tandai "DRAFT — PENDING VERIFICATION".
TIER 3 — EXPLORATORY
Semua sources termasuk D4. Untuk early scan, pattern detection. Jangan jadikan basis keputusan tanpa verify.
STEP BY STEP

WORKFLOW END-TO-END

STEP 01 • 5 MENIT

Create Notebook & Upload Sources

Buka notebooklm.google.com → "Create new notebook" → upload PDF, Google Docs, Slides, website URL, YouTube link, atau teks tempel. Rate sumber terlebih dahulu (framework slide sebelumnya).

💡 Tip Pamen: Gunakan nama notebook yang informatif — "INTEL_LATGAB_INDOPASIFIK_2026_Q1_TIER1". OPSEC: classification level harus jelas. Jangan mix notebook Tier 1 dan Tier 3.
STEP 02 • 2 MENIT

Review Auto-Generated Overview

NotebookLM otomatis generate summary semua sources. Review untuk pastikan coverage lengkap — jika ada dokumen penting yang tidak ter-reference, upload ulang atau re-process.

💡 Kalau overview-nya terlalu generik, itu tanda sources Anda terlalu beragam topiknya. Pertimbangkan untuk split ke beberapa notebook fokus.
STEP 03 • 10 MENIT

Ask Focused Questions (Chat Panel)

Gunakan RISEN framework (Modul 01) dengan mindset: "jawaban harus grounded di dokumen yang sudah saya upload". NotebookLM akan refuse atau caveate kalau tidak ada data.

CONTOH QUERY RISEN (copy-adaptasi):
Role: Senior analyst Koopsudnas
Input: [otomatis: semua dokumen di notebook]
Steps: Extract force posture indicators → cross-reference ke historical baseline → flag deviations
Expectation: Tabel indicators: Indikator | Current | Baseline | Deviation | Source citation
Narrowing: Hanya periode 2025-2026, hanya sumber rated reliable (A1/B2)
STEP 04 • 5 MENIT

Generate Studio Outputs

Di panel "Studio" kanan, pilih output format yang sesuai kebutuhan:

🎙️ Audio Overview
Podcast 2-host 10-20 menit. Customizable focus.
🗺️ Mind Map
Visual hierarki otomatis semua topik.
📋 Briefing Doc
Executive summary multi-section.
❓ FAQ
Anticipated questions + jawaban sourced.
📅 Timeline
Chronological events dari semua sources.
📊 Study Guide
Key concepts + quiz dari materi.
STEP 05 • 3 MENIT

Validate Citations (Wajib)

Setiap klaim NotebookLM punya inline citation [1], [2]. Klik untuk lompat ke paragraf asli di source. Wajib spot-check 3-5 citation acak sebelum forward ke komandan.

⚠ OPSEC: NotebookLM tidak train model pada data Anda (policy Google), TAPI jangan upload material classified di akun personal. Gunakan akun Workspace Enterprise untuk data sensitif. Hapus notebook setelah ops selesai.
STEP 06 • 2 MENIT

Share & Collaborate

Share notebook ke tim (read-only atau edit). Tim lain bisa tanya question sendiri ke notebook yang sama → konsistensi baseline intel antar analyst. Sangat berguna untuk distributed analysis team.

QUERY LIBRARY

5 QUERY TEMPLATE SIAP PAKAI

RISEN-structured queries dioptimalkan untuk NotebookLM — copy, adaptasi nama topik, paste

Role: Senior intelligence analyst Koopsudnas

Based ONLY on the documents in this notebook, extract all force posture indicators for [NEGARA/KAWASAN].

For each indicator, provide:
- Indicator description
- Current status / value
- Historical baseline (if mentioned in sources)
- Deviation direction: INCREASING / STABLE / DECREASING
- Source citation (document name + page/section if available)
- Confidence level: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW

Format: table with columns above.
Narrowing: Focus on period [PERIODE]. Only include indicators explicitly stated in sources — do NOT infer or extrapolate beyond what is written.
Role: Senior intelligence analyst

Analyze ALL documents in this notebook to identify recurring patterns related to [TOPIK/FENOMENA].

Steps:
1. List all mentions of [TOPIK] across sources, with date and source name
2. Identify any temporal pattern (is this increasing, decreasing, cyclical?)
3. Identify any geographic pattern (where does this cluster?)
4. Note any source disagreements or conflicting information
5. Flag what is most significant for Indonesian air defense consideration

Expectation: Structured analysis with pattern statement → supporting evidence → confidence level → caveat.
Important: Clearly state when a pattern is inferred vs explicitly stated in sources.
Role: Military capability analyst

Based on documents in this notebook, assess the capability profile of [NEGARA/UNIT] in the domain of [DOMAIN: e.g., air defense, ISR, strike].

Structure your assessment:
1. CURRENT CAPABILITIES: What capabilities are explicitly confirmed in sources?
2. ASSESSED INTENT: What does stated doctrine/procurement suggest about future direction?
3. IDENTIFIED GAPS: What capabilities appear to be missing based on stated ambitions?
4. TIMELINE: Any acquisition or development timelines mentioned?
5. COMPARISON: How does this compare to [BENCHMARK NEGARA] if mentioned in sources?

Confidence level for each section. Cite sources for all claims. If sources are silent on a topic, say so explicitly.
Role: Legal-intelligence analyst

Review the documents in this notebook to assess the compliance posture for the following operational scenario:

Scenario: [DESKRIPSIKAN SKENARIO OPERASI SECARA UMUM]

Check against:
1. Relevant national regulations (Perpres/Permenhan if in sources)
2. Applicable ROE provisions
3. Bilateral/multilateral MOU or agreements
4. International law considerations if mentioned in sources

Output:
- CONSISTENT: provisions that support the operation
- CONSTRAINING: provisions that limit or require caution
- GAPS: areas where no clear guidance exists in provided documents
- RECOMMENDATION: flag items requiring legal review before execution

Important: Only cite what is actually in the notebook documents.
Role: After-Action Review analyst

Analyze all SITREP, debrief notes, and exercise reports in this notebook.

Extract and categorize findings:
1. SUCCESSES: What worked well? (explicit positive mentions)
2. GAPS: What recurring coordination failures or shortfalls appear?
3. NEAR-MISSES: Any incidents flagged as requiring procedure review?
4. RECOMMENDATIONS: Explicit recommendations from after-action reports
5. FREQUENCY: Which issues appear in multiple exercises/operations?

Output: Priority-ranked issues list (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW) with:
- Issue description
- Frequency of occurrence
- Supporting citations (which exercises/dates)
- Status if mentioned (open/resolved/in-progress)
PITFALLS

HAL YANG HARUS DIHINDARI

🚫

Upload material classified ke akun personal

Walaupun Google claim tidak train model pada data user, compliance regulasi TNI tidak mengizinkan data classified di cloud third-party tanpa izin. Selalu pakai akun Workspace Enterprise yang sudah di-vet dan mendapat clearance.

🚫

Trust audio overview tanpa validate source

Audio overview tone-nya persuasif dan natural — komandan bisa menerima insight tanpa tanya sumber. Berbahaya kalau ada dokumen low-reliability di notebook. Rate dan filter source sebelum upload (gunakan framework di Slide 4).

🚫

Tanya hal di luar sources

NotebookLM akan jawab "tidak ada informasi di sources" — tapi kadang dia stretch dari knowledge umum tanpa sadar. Query harus tightly scoped: "berdasarkan dokumen di notebook ini..." selalu lebih aman.

🚫

Mix sources dengan reliability sangat berbeda tanpa tiering

Kalau upload A1 bersama F6, NotebookLM tidak otomatis membedakan. Klaim dari F6 bisa muncul di briefing dengan bobot sama seperti A1. Gunakan tiering notebook — pisahkan High Confidence dari Exploratory.

🚫

Lupa cleanup notebook setelah ops selesai

Notebook persistent — kalau ops sudah selesai dan datanya tidak lagi relevan, delete atau archive untuk reduce data surface area. Buat log retention notebook seperti log dokumen klasifikasi.

EXERCISE

HANDS-ON 25 MENIT

🎯 Skenario

Anda adalah analis di Pusintelsudnas. Komandan minta "briefing singkat + audio 10 menit" tentang perkembangan latihan udara gabungan di Indo-Pasifik 6 bulan terakhir. Waktu persiapan: 25 menit. Source: OSINT publicly available.

1
Rate & Collect Sources (7 min)

Cari 8-10 artikel OSINT publicly-available (Jane's, The Diplomat, War on the Rocks, Defense News) tentang latihan udara Asia-Pacific 6 bulan terakhir. Rate setiap sumber (A1/B2/C3) sebelum upload. Buat notebook "LATGAB_INDOPASIFIK_2026_TIER1" dan upload sumber rating A1/B2 saja dahulu.

2
Structured Query dengan RISEN (5 min)

Gunakan Query Template Q-01 (Force Posture Indicators) — adaptasi untuk topik latihan udara. Kirim ke Chat panel. Review output, klik 3 citation acak untuk verify apakah benar ada di sumber aslinya.

3
Generate Briefing Doc (3 min)

Di Studio panel, klik "Briefing Doc". Tambahkan custom instruction: "Focus on operational implications for Indonesian Air Force. Use formal structure: Executive Summary → Key Findings → Implications." Download atau copy ke Google Docs.

4
Generate Audio Overview (7 min)

Customize dengan instruction dari Slide 6 (copy dari prompt block). Generate → tunggu selesai → download MP3 → dengarkan 3 menit pertama untuk quality check: tone sesuai? Tidak sensasional? Akurat vs apa yang Anda baca?

5
Reflect & Debrief (3 min)

Jawab di kertas: (a) Berapa lama ini vs cara lama Anda? (b) Apa 1 batasan NotebookLM yang paling perlu Anda waspadai? (c) Untuk jenis task apa lagi ini applicable di ops sehari-hari Anda? (d) Apa yang perlu di-improve di workflow ini sebelum dipakai secara rutin?
